A few things worth knowing

We want you to get the most out of every report — which means being upfront about what it is, and what it isn't.

  • Powered by AI — and AI isn't infallible. The report is generated by language-model agents reading public web sources. They're fast, thorough and improving constantly, but they can occasionally miss nuance, surface a stale figure, or misread an edge case. Always sanity-check anything that would materially affect your decision.
  • Research, not investment advice. PropertyReport is designed to give you a data-rich starting point — comparable to several hours of a researcher's work — so you can make a more informed, calculated decision. It is not financial, legal, tax or investment advice, and it does not account for your personal circumstances.
  • Engage a licensed professional before you sign. Use the report to ask sharper questions of your conveyancer, buyer's agent, mortgage broker or financial adviser. Their job is to help you act on the information; ours is to put it in front of you efficiently.
